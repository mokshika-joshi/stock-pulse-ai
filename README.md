# stock-pulse-ai
Stock Pulse AI is a smart Chrome extension that helps you track and understand the stock market in real time. It provides live market updates, instant stock search with suggestions, and a personalized wishlist to monitor your favorite stocks.

The extension uses Yahoo Finance data to deliver real-time stock prices, charts, and search suggestions. It also integrates Google Gemini AI to analyze market data and generate insights such as market sentiment, risk factors, and buy/hold/sell signals.

With Stock Pulse AI, you can quickly get a clear snapshot of any stock and make faster, smarter decisions—all directly from your browser.
